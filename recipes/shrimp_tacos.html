Shrimp Tacos

Description

Fresh grilled shrimp tacos with lots of flavor and personality

Ingredients

  • Small shrimp
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Coleslaw
  • Pico de Gallo
  • Chipotle sauce
  • Grilled Pineapple (diced)

Steps

  1. Grill shrimp until fully cooked
  2. Grill and dice pineapple
  3. Warm tortillas before assembling tacos
  4. Assemble ingredients in tortillas to your preference
  5. Enjoy :)
