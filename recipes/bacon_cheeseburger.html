Bacon Cheeseburger

Description

A juicy beef patty top with cheese and bacon on a toasted bun

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Ground Beef
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Bacon Strips
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Burger Bun

Steps

  1. Clean and cut tomato and lettuce
  2. Make patty and cook until medium well
  3. Toast buns on the grill
  4. Cook bacon
  5. Put patty on bun with fixings
