Chicken Parm

Description

Crispy fried chicken covered in marinara sauce and cheese on top of spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Breading
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Coat chicken breasts in egg wash
  2. Coat chicken breasts in breading
  3. Fry chicken breasts until golden brown and fully cooked
  4. Cover chicken with sauce and cheese, bake
  5. Cook noodles, serve altogether
